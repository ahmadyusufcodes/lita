Screen Safety Notice

High brightness and static images can stress some screens. Use responsibly to reduce burn-in, retention, and thermal wear risk.

  • Avoid long sessions at 100% brightness, especially on OLED panels.
  • Lower brightness for long sessions and vary temperature periodically.
  • Take regular short breaks (for example every 20-30 minutes).
  • If your display feels hot, reduce intensity and let it cool down.
